Frequently Asked Questions

Q. How long does an assessment take?

A. For ADCS one-time assessments, we deliver reports within 2 weeks from order. Comprehensive penetration testing takes approximately 3-4 weeks.

Q. Can assessments be conducted remotely?

A. Yes, possible. ADCS can be conducted entirely remotely. For on-site visits if needed, please contact us separately.

Q. What payment methods are available?

A. We accept bank transfer and credit card payments (via Stripe). Subscription plans offer monthly or annual payment options.

Q. Are reports in Japanese?

A. Yes, we provide Japanese reports as standard. English reports are also available at no additional cost.

Q. Can you work with small businesses?

A. Absolutely. Avisail is specifically designed for SMBs and small organizations. Many of our clients have fewer than 10 employees.

Q. Is post-assessment support available?

A. After report delivery, we provide 30 days of free email support. For continuous support needs, please consider our subscription plan.
