Intelligence / B2B

AI Infrastructure Risk Intelligence

Challenge: Visualizing "failure risks" in AI infrastructure investments

Evaluating AI data centers and GPU infrastructure not as "investment targets" but as "critical infrastructure that must not fail." Multi-dimensional analysis of credit, operational, and cyber risks to visualize preconditions for investment and business decisions.

  • Operator continuity & dependency analysis
  • Power/cooling/network single points of failure
  • Cyber risk & vulnerability assessment
  • Time-series risk tracking & alerts
Target Clients: Infrastructure investors, AI service providers, Funds
* We do not provide investment recommendations, price predictions, or return forecasts
Intelligence / B2B

Critical Materials Supply Chain Intelligence

Challenge: Quantifying "supply disruption and distortion risks" for strategic resources

For strategic resources including rare earths, we focus on "supply disruption and distortion risks" rather than price or short-term supply/demand. Multi-dimensional risk analysis including geopolitical, regulatory, and environmental factors.

  • Country/region supply dependency analysis
  • Refining process bottleneck assessment
  • Geopolitical, regulatory & export restriction risks
  • Environmental/ESG regulatory constraints
Target Clients: Manufacturing, Infrastructure investors, Policy organizations
* We do not forecast resource prices or provide investment/trading recommendations
