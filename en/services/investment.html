What is Aviscore?

Aviscore is a system that provides "decision-making materials," not "decisions themselves."
It evaluates market conditions, digital/physical infrastructure, and external risk factors surrounding businesses and assets in real-time, visualizing them as status scores.

Status scores (Stable / Caution / Unstable)
